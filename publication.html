Publication

My research work includes publications in reputed journals such as Journal of Intelligent & Fuzzy Systems and Annals of Optimization Theory and Practice. Topics of my research include interval-valued intuitionistic neutrosophic fuzzy numbers, decision-making models, algebra, fuzzy set theory, and number theory.
